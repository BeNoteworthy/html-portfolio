My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Sound of Music

I grew up watching this movie whenever it came on TV. I especially loved the songs, which are classics, as well as the scenery in the movie.

Mr. Holland's Opus

I'm sure you see a theme here! I love music in movies!

Cinderella

Going back to my childhood, the version with Brandy, Whitney Houston, Paolo Montalban, Bernadette Peters and more, really resonated with me. Once again, the songs were always a draw for me.
